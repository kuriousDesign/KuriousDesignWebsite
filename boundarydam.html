Crane Controls at Boundary Dam

Developed software for controlling two 250 ton bridge cranes inside a hydro-electric powerhouse in Metaline Falls, WA. The powerhouse tunneled out of the side of a mountain to create the space where six large GE turbines rest inside, waiting to be rotated by the dammed water channeled just below. The cranes inside the powerhouse aid in servicing and refurbishing these large turbines.
